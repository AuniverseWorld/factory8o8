Questions
FREQUENTLY ASKED
What exactly is an AI artist?

An AI artist is a fully realized musical persona — with a unique visual identity, original music, and content — created using AI tools. Think of it as building a brand from scratch, where every element is custom-designed and production-ready.

How long does a full artist build take?

A typical build takes 2–3 weeks from discovery to launch. The timeline depends on the complexity of your vision and how quickly we align on the creative direction.

Do I own the content you create?

Yes. Once your build is complete and paid in full, you own all the content — visuals, music, videos, and assets. It's your artist, your brand.

What platforms will my artist be on?

We distribute to all major streaming platforms — Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, Amazon, Tidal, and more — plus social content optimized for TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube.

What if my vision doesn't align with your values?

We'll let you know during the intake process. Factory 8o8 focuses on positive, uplifting content. If there's a misalignment, we'll be upfront about it — no hard feelings.

Can I see an example of your work?

Yes — Soraya Kali is our first full build, created entirely through the Factory 8o8 pipeline. Check out the case study above for real metrics and results.
